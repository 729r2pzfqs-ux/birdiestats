Key Head-to-Head Moments

2019 The Feud Begins: A leaked video of Koepka rolling his eyes at DeChambeau walking behind him during a TV interview goes viral, igniting a public rivalry.
2020 U.S. Open: DeChambeau's power game overwhelms Winged Foot as he wins by six strokes, his massive driving distances contrasting with Koepka's dismissive comments.
2021 PGA Championship: Koepka and DeChambeau are paired together amid their feud. Koepka finishes T2 while DeChambeau misses the cut, adding fuel to their war of words.
2021 Ryder Cup: Both join the U.S. team at Whistling Straits and publicly set aside their differences, combining to help Team USA to a dominant victory.
Social Media Era: Their rivalry brought golf into the social media age — protein shakes, slow play debates, and body transformations became as discussed as their scorecards.
