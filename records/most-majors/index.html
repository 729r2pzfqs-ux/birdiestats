Frequently Asked Questions

Who has won the most major championships?

Jack Nicklaus has won the most major championships with 18, achieved between 1962 and 1986. Tiger Woods is second with 15, and Walter Hagen third with 11.

Who has won the most of each major?

Jack Nicklaus has won the most Masters titles with 6. Harry Vardon won a record 6 Open Championships. Willie Anderson, Bobby Jones, Ben Hogan, and Jack Nicklaus share the U.S. Open record with 4 each. Walter Hagen and Jack Nicklaus share the PGA Championship record with 5 each.

How many players have won all four majors?

Six players have completed the career Grand Slam by winning all four modern professional majors: Gene Sarazen, Ben Hogan, Gary Player, Jack Nicklaus, Tiger Woods, and Rory McIlroy.

Who has the most majors among active players?

Among active players, Tiger Woods leads with 15 majors, followed by Phil Mickelson and Rory McIlroy with 6 each and Brooks Koepka with 5.
